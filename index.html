Digital Marketing Proposal for Geonsi Engineering Limited

Objective

The goal is to enhance Geonsi Engineering Limited's online presence, attract B2B clients, increase brand visibility, and redesign the website to improve user engagement.

Low-Budget Marketing Proposal

1. Website Redesign

Cost: K12,000 - K20,000 (One-time)

Includes a simple and professional website design using a CMS like WordPress with basic features such as contact forms, service pages, and SEO optimization.

2. Social Media Management

Cost: K2,500/month

Manages content on Facebook, LinkedIn, and Instagram with 3-4 posts per week, sharing service promotions, behind-the-scenes content, and more.

3. Google My Business

Cost: K1,000 (One-time)

Optimize Geonsi’s visibility in local search results by setting up a Google My Business profile.

4. Email Marketing

Cost: K1,200/month

Monthly newsletters targeting potential B2B clients, highlighting services and completed projects.

5. Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

Cost: K2,000/month

Improve the website's ranking on Google through strategic keyword optimization.

Total Estimated Costs

  • Website Redesign: K12,000 - K20,000 (One-time)
  • Monthly Budget: K5,700 (excluding website)
